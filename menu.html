Hummus is a beloved Mediterranean classic, made with a smooth blend of chickpeas, creamy tahini, fresh lemon juice, garlic, and olive oil.This flavorful dip offers a perfect balance of savory, tangy, and earthy notes, making it a versatile addition to any meal.Served with fresh celery or warm pita bread, hummus is a healthy and delicious appetizer that reflects the heart of Mediterranean cuisine.
Baba Ganoush is a smoky, velvety eggplant dip that is a staple of Mediterranean cuisine. Made by roasting eggplants until tender and slightly charred, then blending them with tahini, garlic, lemon juice, and olive oil, this flavorful dish offers a perfect balance of creamy, savory, and tangy. Often garnished with a drizzle of olive oil, fresh herbs, and a sprinkle of paprika, Baba Ganoush is a delightful appetizer that pairs beautifully with warm pita bread.
